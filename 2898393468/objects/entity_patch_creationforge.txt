entity_patch_creationforge

[OBJECT:ENTITY]

Reactions are generated by scripts/creationforge.lua, each with [FORTRESS_MODE_ENABLED], so no
[PERMITTED_REACTION] is required. Only the two custom workshops need an entity permit (buildings
cannot be Lua-generated). Copy the block and change [SELECT_ENTITY] to support another civ/mod.

[SELECT_ENTITY:MOUNTAIN]
	[PERMITTED_BUILDING:CREATION_FORGE]
	[PERMITTED_BUILDING:TRAINING_FORGE]

[SELECT_ENTITY:VILTRUMITE_EMPIRE]
	[PERMITTED_BUILDING:CREATION_FORGE]
	[PERMITTED_BUILDING:TRAINING_FORGE]
